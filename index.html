A Little About Us

Our Goal

Nest’s goal is to create adults that are responsible and functioning members of society. We also want people who take our courses to have less stress; with more know-how on common problems, tasks, and situations, people will be less worried and will have more time to do things they actually want to do (or, discover joy in doing these tasks).

How?

Although you can certainly take individual courses specific to what you want to learn, our comprehensive adulting course covers most general tasks that typical adults have to deal with throughout their lifetime. We help you learn about problems and how to solve them, as well as help you become aware of resources that may be available to you.

Classes

Full Adulting Course
A six week course that meets twice a week for 2.5 hours—here, we cover everything from dealing with your mental health to home organization to protecting your online information. We have six main categories: wellness, money, DIY, work, relationships, and lifestyle. Each week will be something new, with professional guest speakers and in-class activities designed to help you learn these skills.
Individual Classes
Not interested in taking the full course? No worries! By taking an individual course, you can specifically choose what category or skill you want to learn more about. Meeting times and class durations vary on which class(es) you choose.
